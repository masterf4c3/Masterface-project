Welcome to MasterFace! We’re a team of three passionate creatives with a shared mission: to make the fundamentals of drawing faces accessible to everyone. Whether you’re just starting out or brushing up on basics, our tutorials are designed to be your go-to guide for creating expressive, well-proportioned portraits.

Qusai Ahmad - Chief Content Officer: Qusai brings his expertise in Fine Arts and teaching to the forefront, overseeing the content creation process. He ensures that every tutorial is well-structured, clear, and engaging, helping learners at all levels grasp artistic concepts with ease.

Talal Ali - Head of User Experience: Talal combines his background in Graphic Design with a deep passion for user-centric design. He leads the visual and interactive elements of the platform, ensuring that the user experience is intuitive, enjoyable, and effective for all users.

Yousef Adnan - Director of Technology: Yousef, with his Computer Science background, leads the technical development of the platform. He ensures the site runs smoothly, implements new features, and optimizes the overall user experience from a technical standpoint, while staying ahead of the curve with innovative solutions.

At MasterFace, we believe every artist needs a strong foundation. Our goal is to guide you through the essentials of drawing faces—from proportions and placement to shading and details. We want this site to be the starting point for your artistic journey, equipping you with the skills and confidence to create your best work.
