Course #1: Drawing the Nose

Hello and welcome to this course! In this lesson, you’ll learn how to draw a nose step by step. By the end of this course, you’ll be able to create a nose just like the one you see below. Let’s get started!





Step #1: Draw a circle

Start by drawing a small circle. This will serve as the tip of the nose.



Step #2: Add the Nostrils

Draw two small oval shapes extending from the circle, positioned on either side. These will form the nostrils.



Step #3: Add Bracket-Shaped Lines

Around the nostrils, add bracket-shaped lines (curved lines) that connect to the sides of the nostrils. These lines will represent the sides of the nose.



Step #4: Draw the Nose Bridge

Draw lines from the sides of the circle and extend them upward to form the nose's bridge. These lines should angle slightly, following the natural structure of the nose.



Step #5: Shade the Nose

Shade the sides of the nose, avoiding the center of the circle. Ensure you follow the shape and depth of the nose as shown in the reference pictures. The shading should reflect the natural contours, with lighter areas at the center and darker shading at the sides and under the nostrils.
